Oasys

The decentralized platform for technology in education





We are democratizing the future of education

We believe there is a community of passionate developers, educators, learners, and professionals who are willing to work together to bring new technology into education. Thus, we propose an open source education technology platform – Oasys – that facilitates the development of cutting edge tools and innovative content by crowdsourcing it to the community.

(Coming soon)



Incentivized Community

Our token economy incentivizes students and teachers to learn, as well as rate and provide feedback on the platform's tools and content.

Immersive Learning

Our immersive content empowers users to learn using their senses and bodies in AR and VR.

Personalized Education

Oasys facilitates personalized learning through the use of advanced data analytics that identifies knowledge gaps and provides personalized recommendations.
