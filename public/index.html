Oasys Education

Incentivizing creation of high-quality STEM educational content





Our goal is to increase STEM proficiency in K-12

STEM proficiency is not limited to STEM jobs anymore; STEM proficiency is required across the entire US job market, touching virtually every industry. In 2017, 80% of US highschool graduates did not qualify as STEM competent. We are incentivizing the creation and curation of interactive, STEM, learning experiences which have been shown to effectively improve STEM competency.
